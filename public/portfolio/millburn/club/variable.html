Club Information

Millburn High School is proud of the wide array of clubs and activities offered to students each year. At Millburn, students have the opportunity to pursue a variety of interests within the informal setting offered through extracurricular activities. In addition, the school is always receptive to the formation of new clubs and encourages interested students to submit a written proposal to the administration, draft a constitution and enlist the services of a faculty member willing to work with the club or activity as an advisor. Students who violate school regulations ex: substance abuse, the use of tobacco/dip, and smoking will face disciplinary action and may be removed from student clubs/activities.
